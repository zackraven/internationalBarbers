About Us

A Cut Above, Right on Your Doorstep

Tucked away on Ashley Road in the heart of St Paul's, International Barbers has been a fixture of this neighbourhood for years. We're the kind of place where regulars become friends and first-timers feel right at home.

Our barbers bring genuine skill to every cut — whether that's a sharp skin fade, a precise beard lineup, or something more creative with braids and designs. We take pride in our work and it shows.

No online booking systems or fancy gimmicks. Just walk in, grab a seat, and leave looking your best.
